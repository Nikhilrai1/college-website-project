Welcome to Herald Education Foundation

Herald International College (HIC) founded in 2063 B.S (2006 A.D) under the umbrella of Herald Education Network. It has emerged as one of the best academic institutions in Nepal. It is being run by a team of dedicated educationists, professionals and renowned academicians. Our student's, faculties and staff share a commitment to hard work and to advancing society through creativity and innovation. We are about learning, creating new knowledge and being smart about using that knowledge.
